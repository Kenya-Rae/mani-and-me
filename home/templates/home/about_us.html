About Us

Welcome to Mani & Me! What started as a joyful holiday project during Christmas has blossomed into a vibrant, heart-filled business that celebrates creativity, family, and the joy of personalisation.

This mother-daughter duo, fueled by their love for design and creating meaningful keepsakes, brings a personal touch to every item they make. From beautifully printed T-shirts to personalised PlayStation door signs, unique keychains, and heartfelt gifts, every product is designed to bring smiles and memories that last a lifetime.

But Mani & Me is more than just a business—it’s a story of passion, perseverance, and a bond that inspires everything they do. My sister, the creative mastermind, pours her talent and dedication into every detail, while my niece adds her fresh ideas, enthusiasm, and youthful creativity to the mix. Together, they’ve created a space where imagination knows no bounds and where every customer feels like family.

Whether you’re looking for the perfect gift, a customised treasure for yourself, or something special to mark a milestone, Mani & Me is here to make it happen. We believe that every gift tells a story, and we’d be honored to help you tell yours.

Thank you for supporting our journey. We can’t wait to create something amazing for you!
